The Colour of Love

She told me once that her favorite color was blue. And ever since that day, I haven’t seen blue the same way. It’s in everything now—the sky stretching endlessly above us, the waves that crash and retreat like the rhythm of her laughter, the way the evening settles softly, wrapping the world in quiet serenity. Blue used to be just a color, but now, it feels like a feeling. Like her.

She is the kind of blue that feels like home. The kind that turns silence into comfort and storms into something beautiful. She is the color of open skies and deep oceans, of freedom and depth, of something too vast to fully understand but too breathtaking to ever look away from.

She is not just a color—she is a story, a song, a masterpiece painted in shades of love. And if love had a color, it would be blue. It would be her.

Is this heaven?
